Scoring & Tabulation Logic

Calculation Formula

Total Combined Points ÷ Active Judge Count

The Judge Average is calculated in real-time. The system sums all individual points awarded to a candidate and divides them by the number of judges who have successfully submitted a ballot for that candidate.

Ranking Rules

  • Ranking is determined 100% by the final Judge Average.
  • The system automatically sorts candidates from highest average to lowest.
  • In the case of a tie, the HR panel may refer to the highest individual judge's score as a tie-breaker.
